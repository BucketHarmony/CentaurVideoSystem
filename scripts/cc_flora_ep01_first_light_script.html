Typography

  • Title/narration: Lora (serif, warm)
  • UI elements: DM Sans (clean, friendly)
  • On-screen text: handwritten or rounded serif
  • No monospace on-screen — save that for the data aesthetic
  • Lowercase preferred. "kombucha" not "KOMBUCHA"

Post-Processing

  • Desaturate reds toward dusty rose
  • Lift shadows, lower highlights
  • Soft vignette (not dark — creamy)
  • Optional: subtle film grain
  • Floating dust motes particle overlay
  • All transitions: gentle fades, no cuts
